# State Challenge: Designing Resilient AI Workflows

This repository contains a simplified multi-step AI workflow.

It works in simple scenarios — but breaks under real-world conditions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Your task is NOT to fix the code.

Your task is to design a **state layer** around this workflow that makes it:

- resumable
- debuggable
- reproducible
- reliable under failure

---

## 🧠 Focus Areas

Think in terms of system design:

- What state needs to be persisted?
- Where should state live?
- How can workflows resume after failure?
- How can execution be traced and debugged?
- What tradeoffs are acceptable?

---

## ⚠️ Important

There is no single correct solution.

The goal is to explore tradeoffs and architectural patterns.

---

## 📍 How to use this

1. Review the pipeline (pipeline.py or pseudo code)

3. Think through your design approach
